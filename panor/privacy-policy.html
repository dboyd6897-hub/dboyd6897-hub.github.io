Privacy Policy — Panor

Effective date: April 12, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 12, 2026

Short version: Panor is a fully offline app. Your price data, photos, location, and shopping list never leave your device. We do not operate any servers, have no user accounts, and collect no personal information ourselves.

1. Who we are

Panor is developed and published by Sophus Thymos. If you have questions about this policy, contact us at contact@sophusthymos.com.

2. What Panor does

Panor lets you photograph grocery and household product price tags, automatically extract product details using on-device AI, and track prices across stores over time. All processing happens on your device. There is no Sophus Thymos cloud, no sync service, and no account required.

3. Camera

Panor requests camera permission solely to photograph price tags. Images are processed on-device by ML Kit OCR (text recognition) and then permanently discarded. Price tag photos are never stored, never transmitted, and never shared.

The only exception is the optional Save Training Data setting. If you explicitly enable this in Settings, Panor stores a copy of each price tag photo on your device only to help improve future AI models. These photos remain under your control and can be deleted at any time via Settings → Clear Training Data.

4. Location (GPS)

Panor requests location permission to automatically identify which store you are standing in when you photograph a price tag, so the record is linked to the correct store without you having to select it manually.

  • Your location is read on-device only at the moment of capture.
  • It is stored locally as part of the product record (latitude / longitude).
  • It is never transmitted to any server.
  • Location permission is not required — the app works without it; store matching will simply be manual.

5. Data stored on your device

Panor stores the following data locally in an encrypted SQLite database:

  • Product records (type, brand, model, size, price, store, date)
  • Store information (name, address, GPS boundary)
  • Shopping list items
  • App settings and preferences
  • AI correction history (text only — used to improve extraction accuracy)
  • Price tag photos (only if Save Training Data is enabled)

All of this data is stored solely on your device. Uninstalling Panor removes all locally stored data. There is no backup to any Sophus Thymos service.

6. Third-party services

Google AdMob

Panor displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID) and usage data to serve relevant ads. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time in your device settings under Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalisation.

Google ML Kit (OCR)

ML Kit text recognition runs entirely on-device. No image data or OCR output is sent to Google.

MediaPipe / Gemma 3

AI field extraction uses Google's Gemma 3 model running locally via the MediaPipe LLM Inference API. All inference is on-device. No text or model outputs are transmitted.

7. Children's privacy

Panor is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us and we will address it promptly.

8. Data retention and deletion

All data is retained on your device until you delete it. You can:

  • Delete individual product records or stores from within the app.
  • Clear all AI training data via Settings → Clear Training Data.
  • Remove all app data by uninstalling Panor.

Because Sophus Thymos holds no copy of your data, we cannot retrieve or delete data on your behalf beyond what the app itself provides.

9. Security

Sensitive settings are stored using Android's encrypted storage. The main product database is stored in the app's private data directory, which is not accessible to other apps on non-rooted devices.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Panor after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
